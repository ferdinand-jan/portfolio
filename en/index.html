A 48-hour Game Jam that I made alone. The theme was "Stay Here". I decided to create a sandbox game where the player is locked in a room for 5 hours (5 minutes actually). A 24-hour Game Jam that I realized with 2 friends. The theme was "Teleportation". We managed to make a platform game consisting of one level, where our character can't jump.
